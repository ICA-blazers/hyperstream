Why You Should Use a Planner

Planner: Organization: Write down, assignment, key points about the assignment. It will make doing the actual homework a lot easier. More efficient time management: Using a planner will help students keep track of what they have to do. It will help everything be planned accordingly. Stress reduced: If the student plans out ahead of time and see when everything is due, it will help them to not be as stressed. Writing everything down will help the student feel in control. It helps them see the whole workload but to make a schedule in order to get it all done. Better results: If you use a planner carefully and habitually the student will gain their desired results. Tips on How to Pay Attention in Class

  • Remove any distractions such as cell phones, computers, or any objects that will distract you.
  • Focus on the present: That means no daydreaming. Think about that stuff later.
  • Talk to your teacher about your learning style, ask about assignments and even about extra credit assignments that might boost up your grade a bit.
  • Create a motivation to keep listening and learning.
  • Prepare for class, find a seat where you can fully concentrate, participate in class, takes notes, and even do extra research
  • Develop a routine!
